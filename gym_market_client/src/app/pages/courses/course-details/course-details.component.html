Fitness Coaching Gym Workouts and Bodybuilding

Explore the basics of bodybuilding and learn how to employ a complete gym workout programme in this free online course.

We explore muscle anatomy and composition to explain the science of exercise. We then investigate supplements and show you how to add them to your diet. Descriptions


‘Bodybuilding’ is the use of progressive resistance training to regulate and grow one's muscles for aesthetic objectives through muscular hypertrophy: it helps you build up your muscles to look good. We begin this course by examining muscle anatomy and muscle composition. We then go on to explain the roles and responsibilities of a fitness coach. We identify the most important aspects of muscle growth and workout design. We answer questions like ‘how often should you train a muscle group?’ and ‘how important are macronutrients and why should I understand them?’.
